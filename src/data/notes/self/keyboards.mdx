Boards can be tuned for a desired sound! What sounds good is completely subjective and changes for everyone. 

That being said there are definitely more pleasant sounds than others

### Mods

Stabilizer: Epsi, Holee, bandaid (Be sure to wire balance and lube properly)   [Stabilizer Mods](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4XCErBcn5lc)
Switches: Lubricate and Film (Optional)
Case and Plate: PE Foam Mod, Tempest Tape Mod, O ring [4.5mm x 1.5mm x 1.5mm (Bottom O-Rings)](https://amzn.to/2QkttZO) [3mm x 1mm x 1mm (Top O-Rings, Optional)](https://amzn.to/3eM2aAX)
[Noico 80mil sound deadener](https://amzn.to/2OSZgjA) zip-n-fit/case foam, backeneko o-ring mount, adhesive foam strip instead of pe foam (less tedious and gives great results)
polyfill, [plastilina](https://amzn.to/3QvChpx)
